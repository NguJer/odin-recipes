Pho

Description

In its most basic form, pho consists of a foundation of rice noodles topped with thinly sliced raw beef, which gets cooked when a portion of steaming, spiced beef broth is poured over top.

Ingredients

  • Beef Soup Bones
  • Rice Noodles
  • Beef
  • Fish Sauce
  • Onions
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Garnishes

Steps

  1. Roast Bones and Onions

    2. Roast the beef bones and onions until brown and soft.

  2. Making the Broth

    3. Combine the roasted beef bones, roasted onions, salt, fish sauce, and water into a large stockpot and bring to a boil.

  3. Soaking the Rice Noodles

    4. Soak the dry rice noodles in cold water for an hour.

  4. Cooking the Rice Noodles

    5. Cook the rice noodles in boiling water, then drain the water and add the noodles to a bowl topped with sirloin, cilantro, green onions, and broth.
