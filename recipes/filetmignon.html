Filet Mignon

Description

The best filet mignon recipe with a red wine and balsamic vinegar sauce.

Ingredients

  • Filet Mignon
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Red Wine
  • Balsamic Vinegar

Steps

  1. Season the Steaks

    2. Sprinkle the filet mignon with salt and pepper both sides

  2. Searing the Steaks

    3. Sear the steak on a very hot pan on both sides until brown

  3. Making the Sauce

    4. Reduce the heat to medium low and then add the vinegar and wine. Cook and cover the wine for 4 minutes.

  4. Serving the Steaks

    5. Plate the steaks and scoop the red-wine vinaigrette ontop of the steak
