Chicken Curry

Description

A chicken curry from India that features a tomato based sauce seasoned with aromatic spices and seasonings

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons of curry powder
  • Half a teaspoon of grated ginger
  • 1 teaspooon of paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt
  • 2 chicken thighs, cut into bite size pieces
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste
  • 1 cup of plain yogurt
  • 1 cup of coconut milk
  • Half a teaspoon of cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Sauteing

    2. Heat your oil on medium heat and saute your garlic and onions til lightly brown.

  2. Mix in Seasonings

    3. Stir in your parika, curry powder, bay leaf, salt, ginger, tomato paste for two minutes.

  3. Cooking the Chiken

    4. Add the chicken thighs and coconut milk to the pan and bring to a boil and simmer for 20 minutes.

  4. Serve

    5. Remove the bay leaf from the pan and serve and enjoy!
